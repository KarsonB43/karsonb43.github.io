My Path To Med School

Chemistry

Most medical schools require two years worth of chemistry to be completed prior to matriculation. This includes a year of general chemistry and a year of organic chemistry. In addition to general and organic chemisty, biochemistry is also required by many medical schools and is a topic that is extensively covered on the MCAT.
