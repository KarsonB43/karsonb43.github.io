Chemistry

Chem 107

Chem 107 is BYU's general chemistry lab class. You will explore chemical phenomena through reactions and by collecting and interpreting lab data. This class is only 1 cedit hour but is said to require the amount of effort one would put into a 2 or 3 credit hour class. Lab reports usually take time outside of class to complete and tests are difficult.

Estimated Weekly Time Commitment (Hrs/Week):

8-10

Credit Hours: 1.0

Prerequisites: Chem 106 or Chem 112
